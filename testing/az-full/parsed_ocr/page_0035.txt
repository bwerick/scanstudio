Chapter 1 - Real Estate Law and Real Estate Business
Arizona Revised Statutes and Commissioner' 's Rules set the procedures for applying
for a salesperson or broker license. They are summarized in the chapter and can also be
found on the ADRE website at http:/www.re.state.az.us. Once licensed, every licensee
must notify the Commissioner within 10 days of any conviction, any adverse decision of
a court, or any restriction of a professional license or registration held by the licensee. In
addition, licensees must notify the Commissioner within 10 working days of any change
of address.
In certain cases, the Commissioner may issue a temporary broker's license to a
licensed or unlicensed person for the purpose of completing the existing or pending
business of a licensed broker. Each temporary license lasts for a period of no more
than 90 days, with few exceptions.
NAR is the largest trade association in the world. Members of NAR also become
members of the Arizona Association of REALTORS. do NAR created the Code of Ethics
as a guide to ethical practices by which all REALTORS# agree to abide and to which all
licensees should adhere. The Code of Ethics contains three parts: duties to clients and
customers, duties to the public, and duties to other realtors.
