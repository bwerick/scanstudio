Contract Writing
Prior to activating a salesperson license, the applicant must attend a 6-hour Contract
Writing course. This class covers listings and lease agreements, purchase contracts,
and settlement sheets. These 6 hours count toward the 24 hours of continuing education
that is required every 2 years to maintain licensure in Arizona. For continuing education
tracking purposes, the Contract Writing class is classified as 3 hours of Real Estate Legal
Issues and 3 hours of Contract Law.
Broker Management Clinic
A broker must attend a 3-hour Broker Management Clinic before activating an original
broker license. Also, a broker must attend a Broker Management Clinic before becoming
a designated broker, unless the broker has attended a Broker Management Clinic during
the preceding 23 months. All active designated and self-employed brokers must attend a
Broker Management Clinic once during every 2-year licensing period after initial atten-
dance. Attendance at a Broker Management Clinic counts as 3 hours of Commissioner's
Standards continuing education credit.
License Renewal
Arizona requirements for licensing renewal mandate 24 hours of continuing education
every 2 years during the 4 year license period. The 24 hours must consist of 3 hours
of Commissioner's Standards, 3 hours of Real Estate Legal Issues, 3 hours of Agency
Law, 3 hours of Contract Law, 3 hours of Disclosure, and 3 hours of Fair Housing.
The balance may be of a General category or additional hours of the required categories,
as long as it is not the same class. Self-development or motivational classes are generally
not approved for continuing education. Distance learning is allowed for license renewal
credit.
Reinstatement
Ifal license is not renewed in a timely fashion, it will expire. Paying the appropriate fees and
taking the required renewal classes can reinstate an expired license. A licensee has 1 year to
reinstate the license. During that year, the licensee may not function as a real estate licensee.
When reinstated, the time dates back to the expiration date of the license, not 2 years from
the reinstatement date. A salesperson or broker's license expires on the last day of the
month issued. If an expired license is not renewed within the l-year time period, then
the license is terminated and the licensee must re-test and apply for a new license.
