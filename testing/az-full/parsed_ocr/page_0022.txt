CHAPTER OBJECTIVES
After completing this chapter, you should be able to:
Identify the sources of real estate law.
Explain that Arizona real estate law consists of statutory law and common law.
List the areas of specialization in the real estate business.
Describe real estate brokerage organization and compensation plans.
Describe the requirements for licensure and the exemption from licensure.
List the requirements for obtaining, transferring, and renewing a license.
Describe the National Association of REALTORS* and the Code of Ethics.
